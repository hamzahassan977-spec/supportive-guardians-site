— still wondering?

Questions, answered

Is this the same as therapy? +
We offer talking support and wellbeing sessions — a warm, confidential space to talk things through and build practical coping tools. If you need formal clinical treatment or a diagnosis, we'll gladly help you find the right service.
Do you support couples and families too? +
Yes. Alongside one-to-one support, we work with couples, whole families, and children and young people — each in a calm space suited to everyone involved.
How quickly can I be seen? +
In most cases we can offer a first session within the same week, including evenings and weekends. There are no long waiting lists to get started.
Are sessions online or in person? +
Both. Meet online from the comfort of home, or in person — whichever helps you feel most at ease. You can switch whenever you like.
Is everything confidential? +
Yes. What you share stays private, handled in line with UK data protection law. The only rare exception is where there's a serious risk to your safety or someone else's — and we'd always talk that through with you.
