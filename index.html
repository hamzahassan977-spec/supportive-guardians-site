still wondering?

Questions, answered

Is coaching the same as therapy? +
No. Wellbeing coaching is practical, forward looking support to manage stress, build resilience and reach your goals. It isn't therapy, counselling or treatment for a mental health condition. If you need clinical support or a diagnosis, I'll gladly help you find the right service.
Do you work with workplaces? +
Yes. Alongside one to one coaching, I offer wellbeing workshops and staff coaching for teams and organisations, tailored to what your people are facing.
How quickly can I be seen? +
In most cases we can offer a first session within the same week, including evenings and weekends. There are no long waiting lists to get started.
Are sessions online? +
Yes. Sessions are held online by secure video or phone, so you can take them wherever you are in the UK, at a time that suits you.
Is everything confidential? +
Yes. What you share stays private, handled in line with UK data protection law. The only rare exception is where there's a serious risk to your safety or someone else's — and we'd always talk that through with you.
